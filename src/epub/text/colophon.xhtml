The Inspector General
was published in 1836 by
Nikolai Gogol.
It was translated from Russian in 1916 by
Thomas Seltzer.

This ebook was produced for

The Princess of Orange Receiving Alexander II (1818-1881), Grand Duke and Heir to the Throne of Russia, in the Czar Peter’s House in Zaandam, 17 April 1839,
a painting completed circa 1840 by
Christiaan Julius Lodewijk Portman.
